Calculations

Growth (g/day): ((Current Weight - Previous Weight) * 1000g/Kg)/ Days Between Weights

Calories Received (kcal/Kg/day): ((((BM/Formula #1 * kcals/oz #1) + (BM/Formula #2 * kcals/oz #2))/ 29.6 mLs/oz) + (D5 * 50g/1000mLs * 3.4 kcals/g))/ Dosing Kilograms

Calories Required (kcals/day): Calorie Goal * Dosing Kilograms

Fluids Received (mL/Kg/day): Total Inputs/ Dosing Kilograms

Fluids Required: mLs/day = Fluid Goal * Dosing Kilograms, mLs/shift = (mLs/day)/3, mLs/q3h = (mLs/day)/8
